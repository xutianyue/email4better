About Email4Better

Email4Better helps people worldwide write professional English emails, regardless of their native language.

Simply express your thoughts in any language you're comfortable with, and our AI will craft a professional, well-structured English email that maintains your intended message while ensuring proper tone and etiquette.

Privacy & Policy

Email4Better does not store your inputs or generated emails. All content is automatically deleted from our servers after 24 hours.

Please avoid uploading sensitive or confidential information (e.g., IDs, financial details, health records, client secrets).

Please DO NOT make requests involving hate, harassment, discrimination, self-harm, sexual or extremist content, or anything illegal or against workplace policy.

AI can make mistakes. Please always review and fact-check before sending the generated email.
